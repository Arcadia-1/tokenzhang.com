Token Zhang / Zhishuai Zhang

Ph.D., mainly engaged in research on SAR ADCs, emerging amplifier architectures, and analog/mixed-signal integrated-circuit design, as well as large-language-model-driven automation for mixed-signal integrated-circuit design. His work focuses on agentic engineering for chip design and analog/mixed-signal IC design. He has visited and exchanged at the University of Hong Kong and ETH Zurich. He has received international awards including the IEEE Solid-State Circuits Society Student Circuit Design Contest Award, the ISSCC Jack Kilby Outstanding Student Paper Award, and the ISSCC Student Travel Grant Award, as well as domestic funding and honors including the National Natural Science Foundation of China Ph.D. Student Program, the China Education Development Foundation Endeavor Scholarship, and the Zhihua Integrated Circuit Scholarship.

Under the pen name Token Zhang, he created the WeChat Official Account “张托肯用AI”, Xiaohongshu account “张托肯”, Bilibili account “张托肯”, and GitHub account “Arcadia-1”. He is also the developer of open-source projects including virtuoso-bridge-lite, ADCToolbox, and gmoverid-skill. His public project Virtuoso-Bridge connects LLM agents with Cadence Virtuoso, SKILL, Maestro, and Spectre workflows.
